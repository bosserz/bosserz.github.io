Projects

Spatiotemporal Analysis of Imbalanced Parking Demand and Supply
Spatial Analytics

Evaluate the mismatch between parking demand and supply across a city at different times of the day. By leveraging spatial and temporal data, the study aims to analyze whether specific areas experience a surplus or deficit of parking spaces, identify patterns of imbalance, and propose strategies to optimize parking space utilization. The findings will provide insights into improving parking infrastructure efficiency and reducing urban congestion, contributing to better urban planning and sustainable city development.
Why Bangkok's Traffic is So Bad: Analyzing the Transportation Networks of Thailand's Capital City
Network Analytics Transport Simulation

Investigate the underlying causes of Bangkok's persistent traffic problems by examining the city's transportation networks. Utilizing data from road infrastructure, public transit systems, and traffic patterns, the study analyzes factors such as network design, congestion hotspots, and public transport accessibility. The goal is to identify critical inefficiencies and propose actionable insights to alleviate traffic congestion, improve urban mobility, and support sustainable transportation planning in one of the world's most congested cities.

Measuring Accessibility of Public Transit in London, UK.
Network Analytics

Evaluate the accessibility of key infrastructure points, such as hospitals, schools, and supermarkets, through public transportation systems, including buses, trains, and metro networks. By dividing the city into grids and calculating travel times from each grid to selected POIs, the study develops a comprehensive accessibility index. It further compares public transit accessibility with driving accessibility and introduces a new method combining isochrone analysis and accessibility ratios. The findings aim to provide actionable insights into urban mobility and equity in infrastructure access, with an interactive map for visualizing accessibility across London neighborhoods.
